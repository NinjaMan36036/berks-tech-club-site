Events

The club leadership works hard to book speakers that will present interesting concepts, and prepare students for a career in technology.

Berks Tech club was created in late 2016. Since then it has been active in the Penn State campus community, hosting technology based events and partnering with other clubs.


Our vision for the campus is to provide a place where students can discuss technology that interests them, and learn about new concepts. We do this by hosting events that students will be interested in.


For example, we often host speakers in the fields that students are interested in. They talk about new and interesting technology, and how to be successful in their field.
